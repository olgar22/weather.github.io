Visualization: Latitude vs. Temperature

This plot presents a clear dependency between location of a weather station and the temperature measured. The most obvisous trend clearly shows that the temperature gets higher as we get closer to the Equator (Latitude approaches 0 degrees).

However, we can observe another interesting dependency: the left side and the right side of the plot are not symmetrical, clearly showing that the southern hemisphere is hotter on average then the northern.

And finally, we dont have much data on the extreme left and side of the plot. This simply means that the most southern points (South Pole) and most northern points (NOrth POle) do not have a lot of weather stations and do not provide enough measurements for plotting.
