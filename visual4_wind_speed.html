Visualization: Latitude vs. Windiness

This plot presents some dependency between the location of a weather station and the wind speed measured. The most obvisous trend clearly shows that the observations of wind speed in the northern hemisphere were higher then in the southern hemisphere.

However, we can observe another interesting dependency: the wind speeds are not distributed evenly along the vertical axis. Specificlly we can observe that the majority of the weather stations measured the wind speed of below 20 mi/hr. A very small fraction of weather stations registered higher wind speed, with the highest wind speed of 35 mi/hr when the observation was conducted.
